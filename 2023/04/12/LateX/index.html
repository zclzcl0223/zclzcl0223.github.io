LateX

Posted on 2023-04-12 Edited on 2023-04-18 In Tool
LateX is a typesetting system and also a markup language. It is especially excellent for generating math formulas. This post includes some math formula syntaxes in LateX.

Superscript $x^1$

Subscript $x_1$

Hat $\widehat{y}$

Bar $\bar{y}$

Ensemble ${delta}^1$

Fraction $\frac{1}{n}$

Aproximately equal $\approx$

Time $\times$

Dot product $\cdot$

Belong to $\in$

Greek alphabet $\sigma$ $\alpha$

Sum $\sum\limits_{i=1}^{n}$

Multiplication $\prod\limits_{j=1}^{n}$

Piecewise function $\begin{cases} 1,&y=0 \\ 0,&y\not ={0} \end{cases}$

& means align.
